Have you ever seen a beach with black sand? Because of constant volcanic activity, you'll find white sands and black sands on the island of Hawaiʻi. Punaluʻu Black Sand Beach is one of the most famous black sand beaches in Hawaiʻi. The beach stretches more than half a mile and is divided into three sections: the small Playa Chica flanked by two larger bays, Playa del Castillo and Punta Brava.
